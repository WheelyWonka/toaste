Why is it the best cover?

A wheel cover is an essential piece of equipment in bike polo, designed to prevent mallets and balls from getting caught in the spokes during play. Our premium wheel covers are crafted from durable materials and precisely engineered to fit multiple wheel sizes. They not only enhance safety but also add a distinctive look to your bike polo setup, allowing you to focus on your game with confidence.
